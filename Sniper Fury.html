Sniper Fury is an online Single player vedio game developed and published by Gameloft.

This action of Sniper Fury takes palce in the near future. Technological developement
and geopolitical changes rendered former methods of resolving conflicts obsolete.
Countries, corporation and organization employ services of highly trained proffessionals,
who can eleminate specific target withsurgical precision

Gamplay

The game resolves around machanics common for most sniper games, where the
player has to eliminate a great majority of his targets from long distance. To complete
a task, the player can use a veriety of futuristic gadgets, e.g. a detection device that
will tell the location of evry nearby human, or stimulants, which will boost reflexes
to supernatural levels.

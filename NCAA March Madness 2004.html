NCAA March Mdness 2004 is the 2003 installment in the NCAA March Madnss 2004 series. Former syracuse player Carmelo Anthony is the featured in the cover. Commentry is done by Brad Nessler and "Mr. College BasketBall" Dick Vital as he introduced in the game. This is the first entry in which players can pick their favorite schools. The menus are then stylized in the school's colors, and a cheerleader or moscot can appear on the main menu the shool's fight songs plays. The game plays similarly to NBA LIVE 2004.

Features

The game allows the player to pick their favorite school and run a college basketball dansity for 30 seasons and attempt to lead them to national championships. The palyer can play as a prestigious school or a school of lower prestige in an attempt to bring them into the national spotlight. The game allows the player to creat their own school, but with limited options. The game features all of the major tournaments including the NCAA tournament and Maui Invitational and a Frat Party Mode which is four team tournament for four player. Other modes include mascot games, where the players can play as a school's moscot, as well as Diekie V integration where Dick Vitals offers his take is the features of the game and gives gamers hints in Dansity Modes on recruiting. The palyer can also import their draft class to NBA Live 2004. However, the players are given fictional names after they are imported.

Player Names

Players are not given names in the game because of right of the NCAA to its players, as inluding names would hace required the NCAA to pay their players. Player can still give the players names, ans some players are recognizable, such as 2004 NBA draft prospect Josh Childress(Stanford) Ben Gorden and Emeka Okator(Connecticut).

Extras

Extras in the game features the cameos of NBA rookies Carmelo Anthony, Chris Bosh and Krik Hinrich and rowdy college fans and NCAA head coaches saying "EA Sports, it's in the game". For added realism, NCAA head Basketball coaches offers defensive and ofensive strategies.

Controls

The game is the fist in the series to feature freestyle moves and seperate buttons for dunk and layups and the ability to perform an alley-opps. Also the player can make decision in mid-air and make the adjusted shots.

NBA 2K

NB2K is a series of basketball sports simulation vedio games developed by visual concepts and Release annually since 1999. The premise of each game in the series is to emulate the sports of basketball, more specifically, the National Basketball Association.

The series was originally published by sega sports. The beginning with 2005's NBA 2K6, the series published by 2k Sports. As of 2021, the series consist of Twenty-three main installments and sevral spinoff across eighteen different platform. The official esports league, the NBA 2K league, launched in 2018.

Gamplay

Each installment in the NBA 2K series emulates the National Basket Association, and present slight and somtimes bad improvment over
the previous installment. As such, gameplay simulates a typical game of basketball, with the player controlling an entire team or a select player, objectives concides with the rules of Basketball and presentation resembles actual telvised NBA games. Various game modes have been featured
in the series, allowing for gamplay variety. Numerous elemets of the game feature customizable option. Each game features the teams and players from the curunt NBA teams and players have also been featured, as have Euroleague teams and (starting with NBA 2K20) WNBA teams. Fictional players and teams can also be created and complied.

My Team Mode, which was introduce in NBA 2K13, focuses on building a team of players and competing against the other player's team online.
The player's prymary venue for acquring players for thier team is card packs; the player purchases a card pack, which features rendom items the player can use in the mode, including players. In adition to compiling select group of players, the player can also customize thier team's jerseys and court, among other things. The game mode progressed even further on NBA 2K19, with a my team tournament between the best Xbox and PS4 players for a prizeof $250,000 occuring. other online focused modes have also been featured in the series, such as Pro-Am, which focuses on player building a team togather with their custom players.

In adition of regulation NBA games, street Basketball has been featured in Numerous games in the series. Created players and real players can be used in such modes; additionally, some celebrities have made appearances as playable characters in the series. In the most recent games, the street basketball modes are titled Blacktop and MyPark. Blacktop is structured in the typical style of street basketball. MyPark consist of an open area
filled with players who can join different games on different courts. Sevral games in the series feature a mode which allows the players to hold a
Slam dunk contest.

Sevral games in the series have featured games modes that are exclusive to that particular game. NBA 2K11 featured the jordan challenge mode, in which player are tasked with recreating some of Michael Jorden's most meorable feats, such as scoring 69 points in a single game. NBA 2K12 featured the NBA's Greatest mode, where the player can play with past NBA players, such as Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Julius Erving and Bill Russel. The Playstation 3, Xbox 360 and Microsoft Windows version of NBA 2K14 featured a mode titled path to greatness; similar to the Jordan challenge mode, it focuses on the career of LeBorn James

NBA 2K League

Main Article: NBA 2K League

In February 2017, The National Basketball Association, in conjuction with Take-Two Interactive, announced the creation of an besports league centering on the NBA 2K franchise. known as the NBA 2K League, it commence competiton in 2018. It was the first esports league to be operated by a North American Professional Sports League. The league is structured similarly to the NBA; teams are operated by NBA franchises and feature five professional esports players. The teams compete against each other in the current year's NBA 2K game, and each member of a team only uses their in-game MyPlayer. The league features a regular season, as well as playoffs and finals.

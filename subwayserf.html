    Subway Surf is an endless runner mobile game co-developer by kiloo and SYBO games, Private companies based in Denmark. It is available in Android, ios, Kindle, and Windows phone platforms and uses the unity game engine. In the game, player take the role of young graffiti

    artists who, upon being caught in the act of "lagging" a metro railway site, run through the railroad tracks to escape from the inspector and his dog. As they run, they grab gold coins, power-ups, and other items along the way while simultaneously dodging collisions with trains and other objects, and they can also jump on the top of the trains and surf with hoverboard to evade capture until the character crashes into an obstacale, gets caught by the inspector, or gats hit by a train, at which point the game will be over. Special event such as weekly hunt, can result in game rewards and characters.

    GAMEPLAY

    Subway surfers is an endless runner vedio game. The game starts by tapping the touchscreen, while the jake (the game start character) or any other character sprays graffiti on a subway, and then gets caught in the act by the inspector and his dog, who starts chasing the character. While running, the player can swipe up, down, left or right to avoid crashing into upcomming obstacles especially movind subways, poles, tunnel wall and barriers. By swiping rapidly as speed increases, more points can be acquired. A crash result in a game over, but the player can continue running by using keys. The player can collect various items such as coins, keys, score multipliers, super snaker, jetpackes, magnets, mystry boxs and power jumpers. A power jumber provides combustion by launching up the character, while a jetpack has an ability to fly, a coin magnet that attract all coin on track, super sneakers that gives the ability to jump hiegher and score multiplayer that multiplies the score. Items such as a hoverboard, allows the character to avoid collisions lasting about 30 seconds.

    Daily challenges and weekly challengesgive rewards for unique movements throughout gameplay. In daily challenges, the palyer need to collect letters the constitute a world related to the game, such as "score" and "jump", also known as "world hunt". Mission have verious tasks measured by player accuracy. upto 18 character are unlocked using via coins, keys, in game perchases, collecting specific items or connecting to a facebook account. Most character have up 2 different outfits. Meanwhile, up to 17 hoverboard can be unlock with same methods; each have different abilities that can assist the player. When a game updates to a new location, a new character and hoverboard will be available temporarily until the next update.

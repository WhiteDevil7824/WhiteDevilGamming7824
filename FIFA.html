History

FIFA did not add much other than ability to play with club teams, FIFA 96 pushed the boundries. For the first time real player name by obtaining the fifpro licence, the playstation, pc, 32x and saga saturn version used EA's "vertual stadium" enngin,with 2D sprite players moving around a real time 3D stadium. FIFA 97 improved on this with polygonal models for players and added an indoor soccer mode, but an early pinnacle was reached with FIFA:Road the world cup 98.This version featured mutch improved graphics, a complete world cup with qualifing rounds(including all national teams).

John Motson was the first commentator for the FIFA series, and worked alongside Ally McCoist, Andy Gray and Cliv Tyldesley to FIFA 06 but later returned to FIFA manager 08. Mrtin Tyler was the default commentator for the FIFA series from 2006 to 2020,alongside Andy Gray between 2006 and 2010 and Alan Smith from 2011 to 2020. Drek Rea and Lee Dixon took over in FIFA 21.

by 2000 the FIFA series had sold more than 16 million units worldwide, making it the best selling association football veadi game series. in 2010 FIFA series had sold over 100 millon copies, making it best selling sports vedio game franchise the world and the most profitable EA sport Title. With FIFA 12 selling 3.2 million copies in the first week after 27 September North American debut in 2011, EA Sports dubbed it "most successfull launch in EA Sports history"

In 2012, EA Sports signed Leonel Messi to the FIFA franchise, lurring him away from the compititor Pro evolution soccer. Messi's likness was then immidiatly placed on the cover of street. In 2013, the spanish proffesional women's footballer Vero Boqute started a petition on change.org which call upon electronic Arts to introduce female player in FIFA Series. The petition attracted 20,000 signatures in 24 houres. FIFA 16, released on 25 September 2015, included female national teams.

World Criket Championship

It is series of 3D Criket Mobile Game developed by Nextwave Multimedia. WCC is the first game of the WCC Franchise which was launch and released in 2011. The updated version of WCC2, WCC Rivals and WCC3 were also released under the WCC franchise. As of March 2020, WCC is currently ranked as the no 1 Cricket Mobile Game franchise in the world with 110 million downloads worldwide and with aproximately 11 milions monthly active users. It has reported an avrage of over 2.1 millions daily active users and is one of the most dounloaded crciket game franchise in the world.

WCC 2

WCC 2 was officially released in 2015 and is sequel to WCC. This game has different commentry option in Hindi and English. Being an advanced version of WCC, WCC2 had player customization where users could customize player name, Jersey numbers, faces, etc. They could even change thier squads of both playing and opposition team as well. The game features about 18 international teams, 42 Criket Stadiums, include all three formats of criket (test, ODI and T201). The game features 11 diffrent tournaments including 50 over World Cup, National Premeir league (based over the Indian Premier League), ODI bilateral series, World T20 CUP, Asian cup, Ashes-to-Ashes, and 2 over bitz tournaments. WCC2 also consist of live criket features such as hot events featuring ongoing bilateral criket series and social modules such as Gang of Criket, World tour and challenge a friend. WCC2 also features 3 multiplayer formats such as offline player, online multiplayer and batting multiplayer the game was ratted amongst the top 10 games among monthly active users for 3 successive years fom 2016 to 2018 sience its launch. In march 2020, WCC was partnered with mobile premier league(MPL), one of india's leading mobile gamming platform.


In August 2020, WCC2 won people's choice gaming category in the digital India with 6,607 votes, counting of 74% of total votes in the category.

WCC3 is the fourth instalment of the world Criket Championship franchise. The game was announced in November 2019 amd the beta version was made available in may 2020. The game was officialy launched in september 2020. It includes proffestional commentry from various former criketers in six languages, Matthew Hyaden and Isa Guha for English, Akash chopra and Anjum Chopra for hindi, venkatapathy raju for telugu, Deep Dasgupta for Bangali, Vijay Bharadwaj for Kannada and Abhinav Mukund for Tamil.

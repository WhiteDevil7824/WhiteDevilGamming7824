Sniper Elite 4 is a 2017 Third-person tacticle shooter stealth vedio game developed and published

Windows, Playstation 4 and Xbox One on 14 February 2017 and for stadia on 1 November 2020.
A Nintendo Switch port of the game was released on 17 November 2020.

Gameplay

Sniper Elite 4 is a Tractical shooting game with stealth element, played from a third-person prespective. When the player kills a enemy using sniper rifle from a long distance, the x ray kill cam system will activate, in which the game's camra follows the bullet from the sniper rifle to the target, and shows body part, bone or internal body organs bieng broken or ruptured by bullet. The system is also being expended to include sharpnel kills, melee kills and stealth kills.

The game's artificial intelligence is improved, with enemeies being able to react to the player's action more responsively. When an enemy is kiiled, other enemies will begin actively looking for them. The player is equipped with a pair of binoculars, which shows the player were the enemies are and what weapons they are carrying, as well as the abillity to tag opponents and objects. The game features the "officers" rank. Killing the officer as early as possible will lead his army to retreat, while killing him by the end of mission will significantly increase the level difficult. The player can place traps and use corpses as booby traps. The game also features night missions, in which the player can remove light sources to hide their presence.

Maps in the game are significantly larger than Sniper Elite 3, granting players more freedom and open-ended gameplay. The maps are also features more verticality. The game's new movment system allows Fairburne to climb and grab ledges. Coorperative mission and compititive multiplayers mode are reintroduce.

Plot

Sniper Elite 4 is set in Italy in 1943, in the immediat aftermath of the events in Sniper Elite 3. Upon learning about the remours of a new nazi weapon, the Royal Navy sends a feighter, the orchidia, to the island of scan celni to investigate. The ship is quickly sunk by the new weapon-a redio guided anti-ship messile under the develoment of scientist Andreas Kessler and Heinz Bohm, a high ranking Nazi genral that Allies have little information about. SOE Sniper Karl Fairbune is sent to the island after the sinking and tasked with assassinating General Tobias Schmidt along with sevral other officers who oversaw the attack, filming it to use a propaganda. After eliminating schmidt, Fairburne is sent to the village of Bitanti by OSS agent jack Weaver to find Sofia "Angel" Di Rocco, leader of the local partisan resistance group. Distrustful of the OSS who allowed her father to be kidnapped by Nazi, Angle enlists Fairburne to destroy the railway gun at the Regelino Viaduct in order to prove himself and convince the group to join forces. After blowing up the viaduct and destroying the gun, fairbune is sent to investigate a dockyard that are Nazis are using to ship the weapon part out of and call in a boombing raid to destroy the facility.

Gather intelligence allows the SOE to discover Bohm is aware of operation Avalanche and has the constructed missiles sent to his fortress at Allagra, intending to both infict enough damage on the fleet to prevent the invation of Italy from being a success and attempting to kill Genral Dwight Eisenhower. Fairbune infiltrates the base, disbling each of the missiles. As he attempts to call in a boombing raid on the facillity, Dorfmann appears with a capture Angles, revealing himself to be Bohm. Bohm executes Angles and quickly departs to his plane, intending to take off and personally carry out the attack on the Eisenhower's ship. Fairburne gave chase and succeds in shooting out the plane's engines before it can take off, destroying the plane and killng the Bohm just as a Allied Lancaster bombers arrive to destroy the base. In the epilogue scene, Jack offers Karl a job in the OSS and Karl says he will think about it.

Development

Sniper Elite 4 was developed by Rebellion Developments. According to the Tim Jones, Rebellion's head of creative, the game was designed to be a "Sniper paradise". As a result, the team expended the size and scale of the game's map and levels to make "real long shorts" possible. The animation system, artificai inteligence and the game's rendering technology were also reworked.

505 Games, the publisher of two previous games, is not involved in the game's production and Sniper Elite 4 will be self-publisher bt Rebellion digitally. Rebellion instead partnered sold out and U&I Entertainment, the publisher and distributers of the zombie Army Trilogy, to produce physical copiese of the game. In the February2016, a chinese digital arts company accidently revealed the game. Jason Kingsley, CEO of Rebellion, did not confirm the game's existence, and added that they have plans to reveal a new game very soon. Sniper Elite 4 was announced on 7 March 2016.
on 13 June 2016, at E3 2016, it was announced that Sniper Elite 4 was to be released on 14 February 2017.
